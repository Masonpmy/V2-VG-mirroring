Vocal Mirror v1

Measure your voice — volume, melody, tonality, pace and pauses — and coach it toward the speaking style of communication expert Vinh Giang.
How this works (honest version): this app cannot reach into a YouTube video directly. Instead it analyses your voice and body in the browser and compares them against reference targets drawn from Vinh Giang's coaching framework. Upload one or more Vinh Giang clips — audio or .mp4 video — and the app will measure his voice (and body language, for video) with the same engine and mirror those numbers. Every clip you upload is auto-saved in this browser, and clips committed to a references/ folder in the repo (with a manifest.json) appear automatically. Audio analysis is fully local; body-language analysis lazily loads MediaPipe Pose from a CDN on first use. Chrome is recommended.

What each metric means. Volume = loudness dynamics (great speakers vary light & shade, not flat). Melody = pitch variation in semitones (avoiding monotone). Tonality = steadiness / resonance proxy (a calm, controlled voice vs. a shaky one). Pace = words per minute (estimated from syllable energy when no transcript is available). Pauses = strategic silence — Vinh Giang's "power of the pause."

Reference targets are derived from Vinh Giang's public communication coaching (volume light/shade, pitch range to defeat monotone, deliberate pacing, and intentional pausing) plus general effective-speaker norms — not from any specific recording unless you upload one. Pace and filler detection are best-effort browser estimates. Built as a single static index.html for GitHub Pages. Not affiliated with or endorsed by Vinh Giang.
